About Us

The Gifting Hands Initiative is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the less privileged, orphans, and vulnerable individuals across Nigeria. We aim to create opportunities for empowerment, education, and sustainable living.

Our Vision

To see a society where every individual, regardless of background, can live with dignity and hope.

Our Mission

To empower communities through acts of giving, education, and skill development initiatives.

Our Programs

Community Feeding

Providing nutritious meals to street children and the homeless in local communities.

Education Support

Scholarship drives, school supplies, and mentorship for underprivileged students.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training and financial literacy programs for widows and young women.
